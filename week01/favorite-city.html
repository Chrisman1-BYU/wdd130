My favorite City

My favorite city is Accra, Ghana. It is well known for being one of the most peaceful and well-preserved cities in Africa and the world. Accra is the capital and largest city of Ghana, located on the southern coast along the Gulf of Guinea, which is part of the Atlantic Ocean. In common usage, the name “Accra” often refers to the territory of the Accra Metropolitan District as it existed before 2008, when it covered an area of 199.4 km² (77.0 sq mi). Accra, as a city, and Ghana as a country are beautiful places that many people would love to live in because of the loving and friendly way the citizens relate to and care for one another. .
